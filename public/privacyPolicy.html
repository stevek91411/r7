Privacy policy


Mathspert provides online educational services to grade school students. Mathspert is very sensitive to our users concerns about the privacy and security of their personal information. We are very committed to protecting all data that our users provide during and after registration. We encourage all our users to read this Privacy Policy carefully in order to understand how any personal information collected by us is used.
  1. What information is collected by Mathspert?

    Mathspert requests only a minimal set of information from our customers for registration purposes. In order to register as a Mathspert member parents need to provide their name and e-mail address. For each child registered the information collected is name, grade and date of birth .

  2. What we will do with your personal information

    The information provided is for the exclusive use of Mathspert . The information provided will be used in the following manner.

    • To send a confirmation notice on registration for membership.
    • Upon the user's request, to send the user username and password information.
    • In response to any questions, concerns or problems that the user makes.
    • To contact the user about any problem with the user's account

  3. What we will not do with your personal information

    Mathspert will not sell or knowingly divulge your information to advertisers or any other third parties, unless we are required to share your information with a third party in order to comply with law.

  4. Links to Other Sites

    The Mathspert site may contain links to other web sites that provide complementary educational material. We do not control the content of these third-party sites. The privacy policy of these partner sites may differ from ours and thus users are advised to review such policy information before providing information to third-party sites.

  5. Credit Card Processing:

    Mathspert uses Paypal for payment processing. We do not have access to or store any credit card information provided during registration.

  6. Anonymous information

    Mathspert collects anonymous information regarding the activities of users on our web site. This information is not and cannot be linked to a specific user. It is used purely to enhance the usability of our web site.

  7. Revision of Privacy Policy

    Mathspert reserves the right to change, modify, or revise this Privacy Policy at any time.

  8. Effective Date

    The privacy policy set out above is effective as of Nov 12, 2008. Mathspert reserves the right to change its privacy policy at its sole discretion.

  9. Who do I contact if I have any questions?

    For additional inquiries about the privacy of your information, you can contact Mathspert via email at support@mathspert.com.

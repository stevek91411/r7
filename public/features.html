Parent Benefits
Mathspert is the most cost effective math enrichment programs available. Being web based it eliminates the expense and inconvenience of using a tutor or similar service. Monitoring your child's progress is easy through the rich reporting. In addition Mathspert features no advertizing so you have no worries about what your child is exposed to. The easiest way to get acquainted is to try the demo. It is close to full function and has no restrictions on usage.
Kid friendly
After a few minutes of instruction kids are largely self sufficient. Problems are presented in a familar question and answer layout, much like a textbook. Kids easily navigate through the different problems types and can understand how they are doing from the simple progress meters.
Effective
Proficiency in Math is all about practice and repetition. With Mathspert kids have unlimited access to hundreds of problems types that are targeted at their grade level. Results depend on establishing a routine and having kids do 5 to 10 minutes every day. Real progress can be observed in a couple of weeks.
